REVKIN is a motorcycle group-ride and solo-navigation app for Android. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect and Why

1.1 Precise location (always-on / background)

Your GPS coordinates, speed, heading, and accuracy are collected continuously while a ride is active — including when the app is in the background or the screen is off. This is required for:

  • Broadcasting your live position to your group on the ride map
  • Continuing turn-by-turn navigation when the screen is off
  • Calculating crash impact speed for crash detection
  • Including your coordinates in emergency SOS alerts

Your GPS trail is saved to your personal ride history. It is never shared with users outside your active ride session. Location collection stops immediately when you end a ride.

1.2 Emergency contact (name + phone number)

One contact name and phone number you enter voluntarily in the Safety section of your profile. Used exclusively to send an emergency SMS when you trigger SOS, tap BREAK, or when crash detection auto-fires after a 60-second countdown with no response. Never shared with any third party. Never used for marketing.

1.3 SMS (outbound only — we never read your messages)

REVKIN sends a single outbound SMS to your emergency contact number during safety events (SOS, crash detection, BREAK stop). The SMS contains your name and a Google Maps link to your GPS location. REVKIN never reads your messages, contacts list, or call log.

1.4 Account information

Google Sign-In provides your display name, email address, and profile photo URL. Phone OTP sign-in provides your phone number. Used only to create and identify your rider profile. Your email and phone number are never visible to other users.

1.5 Rider profile data

Bike name/model, rider handle, blood group, and profile photo you optionally upload. Your handle and bike name are visible to other riders in your active session on the group map.

1.6 Ride history and analytics

For each completed ride: start/end time, distance, duration, max speed, GPS trail, and mood selection. Used for your personal ride history, stats, heatmap, and personal record tracking. This data belongs to you and is not shared.

1.7 Crash reports

Anonymous crash reports (stack trace, device model, Android version, app version) sent to Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry for bug fixing. No personally identifiable information is included. Disabled in debug builds.

1.8 Device information

Device manufacturer and model — used in memory only to show OEM-specific battery optimisation instructions (e.g. Xiaomi vs Samsung). Not stored on any server.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not read your contacts list, call log, or existing SMS messages
  • We do not record microphone audio (voice commands are processed on-device by Android speech recognition; no audio reaches our servers)
  • We do not track you outside of active ride sessions
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data
  • We do not use your data for advertising

5. Account Deletion

Go to Profile → menu → Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your Firestore data, profile photo, local preferences, and your Firebase Authentication account. Deletion is irreversible.

6. Children's Privacy

REVKIN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. Contact kumarshivam1993@gmail.com if you believe a child's data has been collected and we will delete it.

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Firebase Firestore data is encrypted at rest. Firestore security rules enforce that each user can only read and write their own data. API keys are injected at build time and are not present in the app binary or source code.

8. Your Rights

  • Access — your data is visible within the app (Profile, Ride History, Planned Rides)
  • Delete — use in-app account deletion (see Section 5)
  • Correct — edit your profile, emergency contact, or bike details in-app
  • Export — use GPX export on any saved ride

For anything not covered by in-app controls, email kumarshivam1993@gmail.com.
