Privacy Policy — REVKIN

Effective date: 15 May 2025

REVKIN ("we", "us", "our") is a motorcycle riding companion app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Phone number — used solely for account authentication via Firebase Phone Auth (OTP). We do not use your phone number for marketing.
  • Precise location (GPS) — collected while a ride is active. Used to display your position on the map, calculate telemetry (speed, distance), provide turn-by-turn navigation, and share your location with other riders in the same group ride session.
  • Ride data — distance, duration, waypoints, and route geometry of completed rides. Stored in your personal account on Firebase Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide navigation, telemetry HUD, and ride logging features.
  • To enable group ride lobbies — your live location is visible to other riders in the same session while the ride is active.
  • To authenticate your account securely.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your location is shared only with riders in the same active group ride session. We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and cloud storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Mapbox — map rendering and directions. Mapbox Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Your ride history and account data are retained as long as your account exists. You may delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

REVKIN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
