WHAT'S INSIDE

🗺️

NAVIGATION

Turn-by-turn routing via REVKIN's in-app HUD or hand off to Google Maps — your choice every time. Cyan polyline, next-waypoint banner, distance + ETA always visible.

📡

TELEMETRY HUD

Live speed, heading, altitude, and odometer. Minimal glanceable display — designed to be read at highway speed without taking your eyes off the road.

🏍️

GROUP RIDES

Host or join a ride with a shareable code. See every rider as a live dot on the map. Host controls the pace — transfer lead or end the ride for everyone from inside the app.

🗓️

RIDE PLANNER

Plan single-day or multi-day rides with custom waypoints. Route preview shows your stops before you leave. Search biased to the area you're looking at — no wrong-city pins.

🆘

SAFETY SUITE

Crash detection, Dead Man's alert timer, one-tap SOS that broadcasts your GPS to all ride members. Voice commands for hands-free control. Weather alerts en route.

🧤

GLOVE-COMS

Large-tile communication overlay for gloved hands. Send STOP, FUEL, FOOD, PHOTO, and custom signals to your pack without stopping or unlocking your phone.

📁

RIDE HISTORY

Every ride auto-logged. Review distance, duration, and route in the garage. Tag photos to waypoints, mark hazards, and replay routes from past trips.

🔑

ZERO FRICTION AUTH

Sign in with your phone number — no email, no password, no account setup friction. OTP in seconds. Your profile, garage, and ride history follow you across devices.
