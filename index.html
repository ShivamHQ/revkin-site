THE PHILOSOPHY

Most riding apps were built
for the commute. Not this one.

The rides worth taking deserve better tools. When you're leading a pack through an unfamiliar state, someone always gets left behind, no one agrees on where to stop, and when something goes wrong — nobody knows fast enough.

When you're riding alone, it's worse. There's no pack to notice you've gone quiet. No one watching your position. No one who gets the alert.

REVKIN runs quietly in the background — the HUD is a glance at 80 kmph, the controls work with gloves on, and the safety net fires whether there's one of you or ten. You focus on the road. It handles the rest.

"The ride you plan for weeks, do with five brothers, and talk about for years — or the one you do alone at 5am because some roads deserve to be ridden."
WHAT IT FEELS LIKE

Less fumbling. More riding.

01

The whole pack, one map

Leave the group chat behind. Every rider's live position is on your screen the moment you start a group ride — no manual sharing, no waiting for location drops. You always know who's ahead and who's two turns back.

02

A glance at 80. That's all it takes.

The HUD shows what you need: next turn, distance, your speed. Nothing else. No menus, no scrolling, no tapping while you're moving. When you need to signal the pack, one big glove-friendly tile does it.

03

The safety net you forget is there

Crash detection is always on. If it fires, your GPS goes to your pack — or your emergency contacts if you're riding solo. Dead man's timer if you need it, one-tap SOS when you don't want to wait. You ride with confidence because the failsafe is silent until it isn't.

04

The whole route, before you leave

Multi-day routes, custom waypoints, fuel stops. Build it once, share it with the pack — everyone rides the same line from the start. No more "where are we stopping?" over WhatsApp at 5am.

05

Communication that works at 80 kmph

A dedicated comms overlay with large, glove-friendly tiles: STOP · FUEL · FOOD · PHOTO · WAIT. Tap once, your pack gets it instantly. No voice notes. No typing. No stopping. Just the signal you need, at the moment you need it.

WHO IT'S FOR

Built for riders who treat
every ride like it counts.

01

The solo tourer who rides into the unknown

No pack doesn't mean no safety net. Your emergency contacts get your live GPS if something goes wrong. Dead man's timer, crash detection, one-tap SOS — the full suite works for one rider just as well as ten.

02

The pack leader who's tired of WhatsApp logistics

You plan the route, you set the pace, you check on everyone. REVKIN gives you the tools to actually do that from the handlebar — not from a 47-message thread at midnight before the ride.

03

The tail-end rider who always loses the pack

You hit a red light, the pack doesn't notice, and suddenly you're navigating alone in an unfamiliar city. REVKIN means the host can see you've fallen behind — and the app can too.

04

The rider who wants their phone to just work

Sign in with your phone number — no email, no password, no account setup friction. Open the app, start a ride, go. Your garage, your routes, your history, always there.
