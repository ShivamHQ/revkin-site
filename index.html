The Philosophy

Most riding apps were built
for the commute. Not this one.

When you're leading a pack through an unfamiliar state, someone always gets left behind, no one agrees on where to stop, and when something goes wrong — nobody knows fast enough.

When you're riding alone, it's worse. There's no pack to notice you've gone quiet. No one watching your position. No one who gets the alert.

REVKIN runs quietly in the background — the HUD is a glance at 80 kmph, the controls work with gloves on, and the safety net fires whether there's one of you or ten. You focus on the road. It handles the rest.

"The ride you plan for weeks, do with five brothers, and talk about for years — or the one you do alone at 5am because some roads deserve to be ridden."

Before You Ask

Quick answers.

Is REVKIN really free?
Yes — completely free, including every safety feature. Crash detection, SOS, group tracking, offline maps, the garage: none of it is paywalled. No subscription, no ads interrupting your ride.
Will it drain my battery on long rides?
GPS tracking is tuned for all-day touring, and onboarding walks you through your phone brand's battery settings (Xiaomi, Samsung, OnePlus, vivo — each one is different) so the OS doesn't kill tracking mid-ride. Location collection stops the moment you end a ride.
Does it work in areas with no network?
Yes. Download offline map tiles before you leave and navigation keeps working when the signal disappears — which, on the rides worth doing, it will.
Is there an iPhone version?
Android first — that's where most Indian riders are. iOS is on the roadmap. Follow @revkinrides to hear about it first.
What happens to my location data?
Your live position is shared only with riders in your active group ride and your chosen emergency contacts during an alert. We never sell your data. Full details in the privacy policy.
