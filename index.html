THE PHILOSOPHY

Most riding apps were built
for the commute. Not this one.

The rides worth taking deserve better tools. When you're leading a pack through an unfamiliar state, someone always gets left behind, no one agrees on where to stop, and when something goes wrong — nobody knows fast enough.

When you're riding alone, it's worse. There's no pack to notice you've gone quiet. No one watching your position. No one who gets the alert.

REVKIN runs quietly in the background — the HUD is a glance at 80 kmph, the controls work with gloves on, and the safety net fires whether there's one of you or ten. You focus on the road. It handles the rest.

"The ride you plan for weeks, do with five brothers, and talk about for years — or the one you do alone at 5am because some roads deserve to be ridden."
WHAT IT FEELS LIKE

Less fumbling. More riding.

📍

The whole pack, one map

Every rider's live position on your screen the moment a group ride starts — no manual sharing, no location drops. You always know who's ahead and who's two turns back. Host can see if someone falls behind.

⚡

A glance at 80. That's all it takes.

The HUD shows next turn, distance, speed. Nothing else. No menus, no scrolling, no tapping while moving. One large glove-friendly tile to signal the pack. Built for eyes that need to be on the road.

🆘

The safety net that never sleeps

Crash detection always on. If it fires, your GPS goes to your pack or emergency contacts. One-tap SOS. Cancel on finger lift. Dead man's timer if you need it. Silent until it needs to be loud.

🗺️

The whole route, offline

Multi-day routes, waypoints, fuel stops. Build once, share with the pack. Download offline maps before you go — no signal means no problem. Scenic routing picks interesting roads over fastest path.

🌤️

Know before you leave

Live weather scored into a single ride rating — temperature, wind, rain. PRIME conditions flagged. Bad weather called out. So you know at home, not 40 km in.

📊

Every ride, remembered

Distance, duration, top speed, route on a map. Your heatmap fills up over time — every road ridden, every route taken. Post-ride recap with mood selector and personal records.

📡

Communication that works at 80 kmph

Glove-friendly comms overlay: STOP · FUEL · FOOD · PHOTO · WAIT. Tap once, your pack gets it instantly. No voice notes. No typing. No stopping. The signal you need, at the moment you need it.
